Who We Are

Former Apple engineers. Passionate problem solvers.

We are a team of former Apple product and engineering managers who are passionate about saving everyday folks money and time on repairs and maintenance.

Repair costs have exceeded inflation by 3x from 2014 to 2024 — and the process of getting reimbursed for money you're owed is fraught with gotchas, UX hurdles, and customer service run-arounds that waste time people can't afford.

Why It Matters

Real people. Real stakes.

If you're a single mom making $75,000 a year with two kids, you don't have time to troubleshoot a broken water heater yourself — nor can you afford to wait to bathe your kids. If English is your second language, finding a compressor part online and installing it in a broken dishwasher can feel impossible. If you're a small business owner, a repair delay can be the difference between a profitable quarter and a loss.

Beyond Savings

Good for your wallet. Good for the planet.

Our landfills are littered with consumer electronics, appliances, and waste that — with better maintenance and preventative repairs — could last years longer. OwnWise helps people fix and maintain what they already own, reducing waste and protecting the environment alongside saving money.
